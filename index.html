A problem in the market

Many existing contract consultancies have a predominately commercial, logistics and fixed plant background with minimal experience in heavy earth moving equipment and direct mining services requiring excessive client input to develop realistic project objectives. This stretching of limited resources often results in executed contracts not fully reflecting project needs.

gdl solutions

GDL identified an opportunity to supply specialist services creating GDL as a ‘one stop shop’ to assist mining companies with our expertise to manage open pit mine mining operational start-ups from the procurement of mining fleet, mining services through to implementation, establishment and bedding in of mining operations.

Our tender evaluation process incorporates project objective criteria and weighting as established by our client’s key stakeholders and assesses bidders based on cost, benefit and risk conformance to the project objectives.

GDL has access to a network of self-driven highly experienced contract and project managers. We pride ourselves on delivering value for money client orientated outcomes.
