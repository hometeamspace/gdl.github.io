Services

GDL's capabilities

equipment management

GDL has experience in, and can assist clients on a short term or ongoing basis with;

  • Procurement of heavy mining equipment.
  • Project feasibility maintenance estimating.

mine management

GDL can supply experienced contract administrators and operations managers (including statutory) to assist in the establishment or the ongoing management of your mining project including;

  • Project management and contract administration.
  • Fiscal management and reporting.
  • Providing short term statutory management coverage.
  • Mine planning and scheduling.
  • Drill & blast engineering.

reliable and efficient

project feasibility

GDL can assist with pre-feasibility studies providing;

  • Ground up mining costs estimating, including fleet simulation and maintenance.
  • Open pit and quarry optimisation and design utilising Surpac, Vulcan and Whittle software.
  • Develop mine budgets.
  • Peer review cost and productivity assumptions.

project development

GDL can assist clients in preparing submissions for mining approvals providing;

  • Project management and coordination of consultant specialists including geo-technical and hydrology reports, environmental reports and mine closure plans.
  • Development of safety management and emergency response plans.
  • Development of safe operating procedures and policies.

quality services

contract tendering

GDL specialises in managing works tenders for mining projects for open pit mining, drilling & blasting, logistics and haulage, equipment procurement and maintenance, tails storage facility construction and mine civil works. We can;

  • Draft all your tender documents
  • Evaluate tender submissions with stakeholder input based on our extensive Cost, Risk and Benefit evaluation model.
  • Negotiate contracts for best outcomes.
  • Provide board recommendations.
